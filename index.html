Our Story

Born from longing for home

Kenmac Fruits was founded in 2023 by Kenneth and Mackenzie — a Canadian food technologist and an Indian agronomist — after they couldn't find produce in Toronto that tasted anything like the fresh, flavourful fruits and vegetables they'd grown up with.

🌱

The Problem We Saw

Over 1.4 million Indian-Canadians live with a constant disconnect: the produce at mainstream grocery stores lacks the flavour, authenticity, and trust of what they grew up eating. Pesticide use goes undisclosed. Farmers are invisible. And the Indian community — health-conscious and food-literate — deserves better.

🤝

What We Built

A direct bridge between certified organic Indian farmers and diaspora families in Canada. Every product tells the farmer's story. Every purchase supports sustainable agriculture in India. And every bite tastes like the real thing — because it is.

"Know Your Farmer. Trust Your Food."

This isn't just a tagline. It's our operating principle.
